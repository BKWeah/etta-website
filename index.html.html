Who We Are

A purpose-driven cocoa trading company.

ETTA Trading Corporation is a Liberian agro-based social enterprise established by young entrepreneurs with a shared vision to transform Liberia’s cocoa sector.

Our focus is practical: empower farmers, cultivate quality, and connect Liberian cocoa to reliable regional and global markets.

What We Do

We help move Liberian cocoa from farm to market.
