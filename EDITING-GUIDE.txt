ETTA Website Upload Guide

Files included:
- index.html
- public/images/

How to use:
1. Upload/replace the full contents of this folder in your GitHub repo.
2. Keep index.html in the root of the repo.
3. Keep all images inside public/images/.
4. Vercel will serve public/images files as /images/file-name.jpg.
5. Push to GitHub. Vercel will redeploy automatically.
